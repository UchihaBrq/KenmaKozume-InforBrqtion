Bio

Kenma Kozume is a fictional character from the manga and anime series "Haikyuu!!". He is a high school student and a member of the Nekoma High volleyball team. Kenma is known for his intelligence and strategic thinking on the court.

Kenma has a reserved personality and often prefers to stay in the background, but he is a loyal friend and teammate. She also known as Nekoma's Brain. She really likes video games(Maybe more then volleyball):)
